## [TR-001 - v1.0.0] - 2026-02-02

### Added

*   **MMG-TR-001: The Illusion of Fatalism** - Foundational paper establishing the Speculative Ontological Engine (SOE) framework's core principles of Agency Depth ($D_A$), Unpredictability Horizon ($H_u$), and Process Sovereignty, refuting Causal Nihilism. Released under CC BY 4.0.
*   **Authorship Transparency Statement (ATS) Framework v1.0:** A technical standard for disclosing Generative AI use in creative works. Includes a six-tier hierarchy (ATS-0 to ATS-5) and a methodology for classifying AI integration. Released under CC BY 4.0.
*   **Compassionate Logic Framework (MMG-GARDENER-1.0):** A methodology for information ethics, introducing the Gardener's Calculus and the concept of Ontological Harm, emphasizing Pragmatic Veracity. Released under CC BY 4.0.
*   **Speculative Ontological Engine (SOE) Framework v1.0:** A research framework for civilization-modeling, treating narrative as an emergent property of complex systems. Anchored by the Antivalent Cycle and the CORE concept. Released under CC BY-NC-SA 4.0.

### Changed

*   Initial repository structure established, including dedicated folders for foundational papers and draft work.
*   CC BY 4.0 license adopted for TR-001 and other core MMG research papers to encourage open collaboration and adoption.
